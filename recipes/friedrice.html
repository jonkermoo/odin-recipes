Fried Rice

Description

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Servings: 8

Fried rice is a timeless comfort food that turns humble ingredients into a dish full of flavor, warmth, and nostalgia. Rooted in tradition and reinvented across cultures, it brings together savory aromatics, perfectly cooked rice, and a medley of textures in every bite. Whether served as a side or a satisfying main, this restaurant-style fried rice captures that signature smoky, umami-packed taste that instantly reminds you of your favorite takeout meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2/3 cup chopped baby carrots
  • 1/2 cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced, or to taste
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups leftover rice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil

Steps:

  1. Assemble ingredients
  2. Place carrots in a small saucepan and cover with water and low boil for 3 to 5 minutes and stir in peas before draining
  3. Heat a wok in high heat, then pour vegetable oil and stir carrots, peas, and garlic; cook for 30 seconds then add eggs to scramble
  4. Stir in cooked rice and add soy sauce and sesame oil and toss rice
  5. Serve hot and enjoy
