Sweet and Sour Chicken

Description

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Servings: 8

Sweet and sour chicken is a beloved dish that perfectly captures the balance of bold, contrasting flavors that define Chinese-American cuisine. With its bright, tangy sauce and crispy bites of chicken, it brings a playful mix of sweet fruitiness and mouth-puckering sourness that keeps you coming back for more. Popular in takeout menus and home kitchens alike, this dish has become a comfort food classic â€” familiar, vibrant, and endlessly satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • water
  • pineapple juice
  • white sugar
  • white vinegar
  • orange food coloring
  • cornstarch
  • flour
  • vegetable oil
  • egg
  • salt
  • white pepper
  • eight chicken breast halves in one inch slices
  • pineapple chunks
  • cut green bell peppers

Steps:

  1. Boil 1 1/2 cups of water with pineapple juice, sugar, vinegar, and orange food coloring
  2. Make a cornstarch slurry, then mix it into the sauce until thickened
  3. Make the batter, then stir the chicken pieces until well-coated
  4. Fry the chicken
  5. Layer the peppers, pineapple, and chicken on a platter
  6. Pour the sauce on top
