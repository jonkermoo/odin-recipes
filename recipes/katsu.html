Chicken Katsu

Description

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Servings: 4

Chicken Katsu is a crispy, golden-fried dish that blends Japanese culinary precision with comforting, familiar flavors. With its satisfying crunch and tender, juicy interior, it’s a beloved staple in bento boxes and casual meals alike. Often paired with tangy tonkatsu sauce, this dish delivers a perfect balance of richness and brightness — simple yet irresistibly craveable. It’s a go-to comfort food that feels both hearty and refined.

Ingredients:

  • four skinless chicken breast halves
  • seasonings
  • flour
  • egg
  • panko crumbs
  • oil

Steps:

  1. Season chicken breasts on both sides
  2. Place flour, beaten eggs, and crumbs in a separate dish
  3. Coat chicken in flour and dip it into the eggs and press it into the panko crumbs
  4. Fry the chicken in oil until golden brown
